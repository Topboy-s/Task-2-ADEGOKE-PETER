Quick Care Tips 🌱

Water when the top inch of soil feels dry, not on a schedule.

Most houseplants prefer bright indirect light, not direct sun.

Yellow leaves = overwatering. Brown tips = underwatering.

Wipe dusty leaves with a damp cloth once a month.

Beginner Picks 🏆

Pothos — practically immortal.

Snake Plant — ignore it, it thrives.

ZZ Plant — low light, low water.

Spider Plant — grows babies you can give to friends!
